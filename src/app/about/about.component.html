About Us

A group consisting of four CSCI499-02 students founded on September 18, 2017
and situated in Hunter North: Room C107. Under the brand of ConCat, the team
was tasked with the construction of the " [Con]ceptualizer and [Cat]egorizer ":
a tool for reviewing a data set to identify its relevant concepts and categories.
